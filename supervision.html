Collaboration Opportunities

We offer to supervise MSc projects on a variety of topics such as MPC, hybrid systems, optimization, vehicle control, etc. To do so, we will first have a discussion to see if there is a possibility of finding a shared interest as a topic. If successful, that discussion will be followed by couple of meetings to define the project clearly.

You can send me an email, explaining the status of your MSc education, your experience and topics of interest. Attaching your CV is recommended.
