how to get there?

What happens during the ceremony?

I will give a 15-minute presentation for the public at 9:30

The committee members join at 10:00 and we start the scientific discussions

The discussion stops at 11:00 and the commitee leave to discuss privately for 15 minutes

If they agree on awarding the doctorate, this happens at 11:15

We will all head to the reception!
