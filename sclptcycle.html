About SclptCycle

SclptCycle is a party on a bike! 🥳

It's an energetic, technique-focused bodyshape workout, designed to improve coordination, rhythm, endurance, and full-body engagement.

I blend choreography with gentle, body-awareness cues and simple physics insights, helping you notice how each motion feels and how your body responds. My goal is to create an inclusive, fun, and uplifting environment where you can challenge yourself safely and confidently.
